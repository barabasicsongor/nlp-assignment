             <paragraph>Master of Software Engineering 
              EndofSemester Presentation</paragraph>

             "T2: The End of the Beginning"
             

                 by the MSE Studio Team

                  SEI A104 (video room)        
                    Wednesday, May 5
                     1:30  <stime>3:00 pm</stime>
The MSE Studio Team will relate its spring semester experience
with the T2 project  providing software for the Field Robotics
Center's Tessellator robot. The Tessellator is designed to service
the Space Shuttles' Thermal Protection System. 

<paragraph>The team formulated the requirements and drafted designs for
both the HighLevel Controller (HLC) and the EndEffector
Positioning Software (EEPS). These activities mark the halfway
point of the T2 project. </paragraph>

<paragraph>The presentation will include the following topics:</paragraph>

 T2 Past and Future.

  An overview of the events of the spring and an outlook
  on the summer semester.

 <sentence>EEPS and HLC Requirements. </sentence>

  The functionality of the software and the techniques applied
  to elicit and analyze the requirements. 

 <sentence>EEPS and HLC Software Architectures. </sentence>

  The proposed highlevel designs for the software and the
  process that led to the architectures.

 <sentence>T2  Process Handbook. </sentence>

  A set of management practices adopted by the team and collected
  for use by other software development teams.

 <sentence>T2 Time Expenditures. </sentence>

  <sentence>Some statistics on how the T2 developers spent their time. </sentence>
